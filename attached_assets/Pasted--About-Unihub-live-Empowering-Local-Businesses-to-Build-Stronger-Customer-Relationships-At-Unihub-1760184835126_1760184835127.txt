🟣 About Unihub.live
Empowering Local Businesses to Build Stronger Customer Relationships

At Unihub.live, we believe that every visit matters. Our mission is to help cafés, bars, salons, and local shops connect with their customers in smarter, more engaging ways — without complicated apps or expensive systems.

Through our simple, QR-powered tools, any business can launch a digital loyalty card, create a custom reward system, or even set up a menu and prize wheel — all from one easy dashboard.

No coding. No installations. Just instant engagement that your customers love.

💡 Our Vision

To make digital customer engagement accessible to every local business, regardless of size or tech experience.
We envision a world where every café, bakery, or boutique can deliver a seamless digital experience to customers — effortlessly.

⚙️ What We Do

Unihub.live provides a merchant dashboard that allows you to:

🎟️ Create digital loyalty cards to reward repeat customers.

🎰 Set up fun, interactive prize wheels to drive excitement and return visits.

🍽️ Design and share your own menu online — complete with a QR code for instant access.

💳 Accept secure payments via Stripe (no hassle, no extra setup).

📊 Track engagement and performance with built-in analytics.

Everything is web-based and mobile-friendly — so both you and your customers can use it instantly through a simple QR scan.
