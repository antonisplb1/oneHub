✅ Step 1: Define Your Cookie Categories

Here’s how to structure the cookies for Unihub.live — short, compliant, and human-friendly.

Category	Purpose	Examples	Consent Needed	Essential
Session Cookies	Keep users logged in and secure sessions while navigating the dashboard.	session_id, auth_token	❌ No (strictly necessary)	✅ Yes
Stripe Cookies	Handle checkout, fraud prevention, and secure payments via Stripe.	_stripe_mid, _stripe_sid, __stripe_orig_props	❌ No (strictly necessary for payments)	✅ Yes
Analytics Cookies	Measure site performance and understand user behavior to improve the platform.	_ga, _gid, _clck (for Google Analytics or Microsoft Clarity)	✅ Yes (user must opt in)	❌ No
Marketing / Tracking Cookies	Personalize marketing campaigns or retargeting (e.g., Facebook Pixel).	_fbp, _ttq (TikTok), _gcl_au (Google Ads)	✅ Yes (user must opt in)	❌ No
